building performance

This project was based around the movements and motions of a given kitchen utensil. The utensil’s movements were first observed, then translated/abstracted into 2D draftings on vellum. The drawings were, again, abstracted into five different sections and finally transformed into 3D wooden models using minimal glue and effective joinery.

MODEL MADE IN COLLABORATION WITH YOOJIN KIM

 
 

The tools implemented into this design are an iron and a potato peeler. The design itself was inspired by the action verb “sliding”, as both of the utensils had a major idea of sliding.
