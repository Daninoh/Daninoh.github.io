Everything in Between

2017



Everything in Between is a book currently being written by Zoey Ryu, a Korean-American poet, writer, aspiring law student, and student at Claremont McKenna College. Zoey commissioned me to create illustrations for each chapter of her book, as well as format it for her. Everything in Between is a "a short book about [her] experience living at the Incheon Airport".
