Foodies

2017



These series of characters were designed for my One Word challenge based on the word "Sandwich". The characters' personalities are based on a couple of my friends. Originally, they were created to be characters for a comic series, but that idea was scrapped after a couple of weeks. The New Years party was commissioned by a friend over New Years of 2017 to "draw [her] a broccoli and pickle party" and honestly... I was very bored...
