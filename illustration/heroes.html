Heroes

2018



This series of illustrations was created to depict a few of the most noteworthy and inspirational architects. From Zaha Hadid and Heydar Aliyev Center to Alvar Aalto and Säynätsalo Town Hall, the architects are paired with one of their most iconic designs, as well as the most iconic pieces of architecture in history.
