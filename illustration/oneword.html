One Word

2017



This select collection of illustrations were done in collaboration with two friends, Ryu Kondrup and Colleen Duong. A single worded prompt was chosen weekly which were then illustrated by each person. The One Word project was done to help improve our quick design skills, proficiency of Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, and overall composition in design.
