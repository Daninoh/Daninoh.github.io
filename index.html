Hi there! I'm Daniel. I am an incoming PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania working with Yasmin Kafai on informal learning spaces. Currently, as a Teaching Fellow at the Harvard Graduate School of Education, I help develop and teach courses on transformative educational technologies, multi-modal learning analytics, digital fabrication and making, and designing learning spaces. I have previously worked in/with museums (Carnegie Museum of Natural History, MIT Museum, Design Museum Everywhere) as a learning designer and educator, where I have designed and facilitated a collection of STEAM workshops, drop-ins, and educational media for learners of all ages and backgrounds.

I hold a Bachelor of Architecture (Honors) with minors in Human-Computer Interaction and Design for Learning from Carnegie Mellon University, and a Master of Education in Learning Design, Innovation, and Technology from Harvard University.
