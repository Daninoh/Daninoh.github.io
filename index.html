Hello, I'm Daniel Noh [also 노주현]. I'm a designer, researcher, maker, and educator currently exploring learning, equity, technology, and creativity.

I am currently pursuing a Master of Education in Learning Design, Innovation, and Technology at the Harvard Graduate School of Education. I am also a Research Assistant at Project Zero's Designing Learning Spaces Lab under Dr. Daniel Wilson and a Teaching Fellow for Digital Fabrication and Making in Education taught by Prof. Bertrand Schneider.

Currently, I'm also building a portfolio for a course based in the Teaching and Learning Lab taught by Dr. William Wisser.
