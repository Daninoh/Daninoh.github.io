Harvard Graduate School of Education, TLL Practicum



This is a living collection of reflections from the Teaching and Learning Lab Practicum (EDU T127) course facilitated by Dr. Bill Wisser at the Harvard Graduate School of Education. The collection will exhibit project updates, thoughts on readings, connections to other courses, and other reflective thoughts from the practicum.

"The TLL advances the teaching and impact mission of the HGSE through the creation - in close partnership with faculty members and teaching teams - of innovative, research-based teaching assets and learning experiences."
